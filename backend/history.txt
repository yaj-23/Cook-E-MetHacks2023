

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 cup sugar

3 tablespoons cocoa powder

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup milk

1/4 cup vegetable oil

2 eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 12-cup muffin pan.

2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Stir in chocolate chips.

3. Pour batter into prepared muffin pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean. Let cool before serving.
A chocolate chip muffin is a type of muffin that is made with chocolate chips. It is a popular breakfast food and snack food, often served with coffee or tea. Chocolate chip muffins are typically made with flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, salt, milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. They are often made with chocolate chips, but they can also be made with other types of chocolate, such as cocoa nibs or chopped bars of dark chocolate.
